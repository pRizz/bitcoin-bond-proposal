## What This Would Actually Do

HB1217 would create the Bitcoin Strategic Reserve Fund in the state treasury and authorize the State Treasurer to receive bitcoin donations, hold the reserve bitcoin for at least five years, and manage the fund through custody, audit, and reporting procedures. The bill also separately authorizes the Treasurer to invest, purchase, and hold cryptocurrency using state funds and requires governmental entities to accept approved cryptocurrency for state payments.

## Key Statutory Mechanics

- Creates the Bitcoin Strategic Reserve Fund in the state treasury.
- Allows the Treasurer to accept gifts, grants, donations, bequests, or devises of bitcoin from eligible Missouri residents or governmental entities.
- Requires bitcoin entering state custody to be held for at least five years before transfer, sale, appropriation, or conversion.
- Requires secure storage policies, regular audits, and biennial public reporting.
- Separately authorizes the Treasurer to invest, purchase, and hold cryptocurrency using state funds.
