## What This Would Actually Do

HB1844 would create a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve Fund in the State treasury and give the State Treasurer authority to hold donated or granted bitcoin in that fund. The bill would require bitcoin entering State custody to be held for at least five years before transfer, sale, appropriation, or conversion is allowed, and it would require management, security, and reporting provisions.

## Bond / Reserve Analysis

**Supports:** direct reserve accumulation  
**Bond-financed reserve accumulation:** no meaningful support  
**Meaningful reading:** reserve-only

HB1844 is important because it creates a direct reserve framework. It is limited because it does not create a bond-financing mechanism and does not make bond-funded accumulation part of the Illinois legislative record.

## Key Statutory Mechanics

- Creates a new Strategic Bitcoin Reserve Act.
- Creates a special fund in the State treasury for holding bitcoin as a financial asset.
- Allows the State Treasurer to accept gifts, grants, and donations of bitcoin.
- Requires a minimum five-year holding period before later transfer, sale, appropriation, or conversion.
- Includes fund-management, security, reporting, and rulemaking provisions.
