## What This Would Actually Do

SB 352 would have created the bitcoin and digital assets reserve fund in the Kansas state treasury. The official bill text assigns administration of the fund to the State Treasurer and defines digital assets broadly enough to include bitcoin, cryptocurrencies, and other digital-only assets.
