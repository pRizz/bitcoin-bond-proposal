## What This Would Actually Do

HB 1155 would have amended South Dakota public-funds investment authority to permit the State Investment Council to invest in Bitcoin. The official introduced text capped Bitcoin exposure at ten percent of state moneys made available for investment and allowed holdings directly, through a qualified custodian, or through an exchange-traded product.

## Key Statutory Mechanics

- Would cap Bitcoin investment at ten percent of state moneys made available for investment.
- Would allow custody directly through a secure custody solution, through a qualified custodian, or through an exchange-traded product.
- Was deferred to the 41st legislative day by House Commerce and Energy on February 6, 2026.

## Why This Is Limited

HB 1155 is credible official evidence that South Dakota considered State Investment Council Bitcoin authority, but it did not become law. The record should not be treated as enacted authority, a live reserve program, or a bond-financed reserve proposal.
