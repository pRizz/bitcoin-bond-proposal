# Illinois Bitcoin Reserve and Bond Act (Draft)

> Draft working text for policy development. This document is illustrative and not legal advice.

## Section 1. Short Title

This Act may be cited as the Illinois Bitcoin Reserve and Bond Act.

## Section 2. Legislative Findings

The General Assembly finds that:

1. Illinois should evaluate strategic treasury policies that may improve long-term fiscal resilience.
2. Bitcoin-related reserve or bond programs require explicit governance, disclosure, and oversight.
3. Any such program should be adopted only through clear statutory authority and reviewable public safeguards.

## Section 3. Definitions

This draft should eventually define, at minimum:

- Bitcoin
- strategic reserve
- qualified custodian
- Bitcoin-backed bond
- responsible state authority

## Section 4. Strategic Reserve Authorization

This section should eventually specify:

- whether Illinois may acquire Bitcoin directly or through a defined mechanism;
- who may authorize acquisition;
- custody, audit, and reporting duties;
- allocation caps and prohibited uses.

## Section 5. Bitcoin-Backed Bond Authority

This section should eventually specify:

- what category of bond instrument is authorized;
- what disclosures are mandatory;
- what fiscal and legal reviews must occur before issuance;
- how proceeds, collateral, or linked exposure are defined.

## Section 6. Transparency and Reporting

This section should require:

- regular public reporting;
- disclosure of reserve policy changes;
- plain-language summaries suitable for legislative review.

## Section 7. Rulemaking and Implementation

This section should identify the responsible implementation authority and any rulemaking limits or deadlines.

## Drafting Notes

- Final bill text should be reviewed by Illinois legislative drafting staff and qualified counsel.
- Bond language should be narrowed only after state-specific constitutional and securities analysis.
- This draft is meant to preserve structure, sectioning, and print readiness while substantive legal detail is refined in Phase 2.
