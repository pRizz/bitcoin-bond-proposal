| Interaction | Contract |
|-------------|----------|
| Catalog filtering | Type filter must update visible card count immediately and keep the empty state readable. No filtered result may create horizontal overflow or collapse the filter controls. |

| Card navigation | `StateCard` hover/focus preload behavior remains. Focus-visible rings must remain visible against the dark canvas, and every card opens `/states/${slug}`. |
